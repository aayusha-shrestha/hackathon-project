Nepal's primary national suicide prevention helpline is 1166, a free, four-digit number operational 24/7 since May 2021, managed by Transcultural Psychosocial Organization (TPO) Nepal with support from the Mental Hospital in Lagankhel and the World Health Organization.  This service provides immediate crisis intervention, counseling, and follow-up care for individuals at risk of suicide or experiencing severe mental distress across all 77 districts. 

Multiple free and confidential helplines are available for specific needs, including depression, anxiety, and LGBTQIA+ support:

TPO Nepal: Call 1660-0102005 or text 9847386158 (8 am to 8 pm daily) for emotional support regarding anxiety, depression, bullying, and self-harm. 
Tribhuvan University Teaching Hospital (TUTH): The suicide prevention line 1660-0121600 and psychiatry help line 9841630430 offer 24-hour assistance. 
CMC Nepal: Call 1660-0185080 for free support with anxiety, depression, loneliness, and stress. 
Koshish: Call 1660-0122322 for mental health and psychosocial support. 
Blue Diamond Society: Call 15547460 (by appointment) for LGBTQIA+ specific psychological counseling. 
Kanti Children Hospital: The child psychiatric help line is 9808522410. 
General Emergency: Dial 100 (Police) or 112 for immediate emergency assistance.
